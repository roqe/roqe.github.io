I am a postdoctoral fellow at the Institute of Statistical Science, Academia Sinica. Before that, I obtained my PhD in Bioinformatics at TIGP (Taiwan International Graduate Program). My recent research interests focus on the causal assumptions for statistical identification. I also work on statistical genetics, causal mediation analysis, and the development of bioinformatic algorithms. A full publication list is available at Google Scholar; discussion and collaboration on research are welcome.
